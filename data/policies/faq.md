# Frequently Asked Questions — Gaiytri

## 1. What does Gaiytri specialize in?
Gaiytri designs intelligent automation systems that help businesses streamline operations, reduce manual effort, and grow smarter. Our expertise spans AI workflow automation, chatbot development, custom AI solutions, and AI-powered web services.

---

## 2. How is Gaiytri different from other AI companies?
Unlike firms that focus on isolated tools, Gaiytri builds a holistic AI ecosystem tailored to each business. We blend technology with human-centered design — creating systems that are scalable, intuitive, and ethically developed.

---

## 3. What industries does Gaiytri serve?
We currently serve clients in **e-commerce**, **hospitality and café management**, and **career-tech** (student job application automation). However, our solutions are **industry-agnostic** and adaptable to any organization seeking intelligent automation.

---

## 4. Can Gaiytri integrate AI into existing systems?
Yes. Our engineers specialize in API integrations, database connectivity, and hybrid system design. Whether you use Shopify, WordPress, or custom ERP systems, we can embed AI tools without disrupting existing workflows.

---

## 5. Does Gaiytri build chatbots for specific use cases?
Absolutely. We develop chatbots for **customer support**, **order management**, **lead generation**, and **internal operations**. Each chatbot understands context, intent, and business rules — creating natural, goal-driven conversations.

---

## 6. How does pricing work for Gaiytri’s services?
We follow a **custom-quote model** based on project scope, integrations, and support requirements. Each client receives a tailored proposal after a discovery consultation. Product pricing will be fixed and published upon launch.

---

## 7. Do you offer pilot programs or free consultations?
Yes. Gaiytri offers **initial consultations** and **pilot phases** for eligible projects. This helps businesses test feasibility and measure early results before committing to full-scale deployment.

Our core tech stack includes **React**, **Node.js**, **Python**, **TypeScript**, and frameworks such as **LangChain**, **Firebase**, **Supabase**, and **Django** (for certain projects). We focus on combining modern web and AI tools for performance and flexibility.

Yes. Data security and compliance are core principles at Gaiytri. We follow best practices for encryption, data handling, and API security to ensure that client information remains confidential and protected.
